1. PROJECT OVERVIEW  
───────────────────────────────────────────────  

This proposal outlines the construction of a 20 x 20 composite deck with medium elevation (4-8 ft) above ground. The deck will include railings on all sides, two sets of stairs, and built-in lighting features. The area is clear of vegetation or obstacles, and the project will involve the removal of an existing deck prior to construction.  

───────────────────────────────────────────────  
2. SERVICES INCLUDED  
───────────────────────────────────────────────  

• Removal of the existing deck to prepare the area for construction  
• Construction of a 20 x 20 composite deck with low-maintenance, modern surface materials  
• Installation of railings on all sides, using wooden railing matching the deck surface  
• Installation of two sets of stairs for access  
• Integration of built-in lighting features for enhanced usability and aesthetics  
• Assessment of slope and gentle grading adjustments as needed  
• Professional advice on concrete footings if required  

───────────────────────────────────────────────  
3. MATERIALS INCLUDED  
───────────────────────────────────────────────  

• Composite decking materials (low maintenance, modern)  
• Wooden railing materials matching the deck surface  
• Lighting fixtures for built-in deck lighting  
• Stairs materials (two sets)  

───────────────────────────────────────────────  
4. VISIT SCHEDULE / TIMELINE  
───────────────────────────────────────────────
